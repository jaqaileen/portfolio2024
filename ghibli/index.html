Ghibli Studio

I have always admired Studio Ghibli's whimsical and nostalgic aesthetic. I wanted to challenge myself to convey the animation studio's warm, magical, and playful aesthetic through a typographic book. The selected text is drawn from several Wikipedia entries on the animation studio, popular works, and the founders. The book is meant for a general audience and contains several interactive elements to encapsulate the childlike wonder of Ghibli films. It is binded in a classic Japanese binding as an homage to the animation studio.

Made of: Mohawk Loop Milkweed Vellum 110C, Colorplan Natural 135 gsm, Reich Paper Clear, & Embroidery thread
